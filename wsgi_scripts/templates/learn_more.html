About Ocean

The Foundations of Ocean

Ocean was created by DCC, Inc., a partnership of four sexy young web developers: Richie, Joey P., CC, and Ringo Starr. The group decided to go about creating the site based on an idea for a completely anonymous, no-strings-attached messaging pool they called "Message In A Bottle". As the idea evolved, a chat aspect was integrated into the user experience, and the project was renamed theocean. However, at the advice of Justin Timberlake in the film The Social Network, the name was again changed to the sleeker, cleaner Ocean.

There was work ahead, but the four heroes knew that they could accomplish anything with Richie's young money millions, Joey's neuroticism, CCs itchiness, and Ringo's extensive experience developing Certified Safe Spaces (CSS).

Ocean's Features

Currently, Ocean doesn't have that many features, and basically consists only of a login and a page where users can create accounts. However, it has recently been transferred to a Flask web framework, and many new features are currently in development. More on this later.
